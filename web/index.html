Features

Everything built in.

No tools to stitch together. No agency to hire.

🎨

AI-generated visuals

Cinematic, cohesive images generated once per song. Every slide matches the arc — hook, tension, peak, release, aftermath, CTA.

✍️

Hook variant testing

Three text styles per post — direct lyrics, emotional reaction, minimalist mystery. The AI picks the best based on your history.

🔗

Smart link page

A beautiful landing page showing all your streaming platforms. Every click is tracked back to the exact post that drove it.

📊

Streaming CTR learning

RunSound tracks streaming clicks per post via UTM, computes your streamingCTR, and self-optimises the strategy daily.

📬

TikTok draft delivery

Posts go straight to your TikTok drafts via the official API. You stay in control — just add your song and publish.

🌍

Multi-campaign support

Running multiple singles at once? RunSound handles them all, with separate learning loops per song.
