FAQ

Questions.

Do I need to post myself, or does RunSound do it? +
TikTok's terms require a human to confirm each post. RunSound puts a finished, ready-to-go draft in your TikTok inbox. You open it, tap your song, and hit publish — about 30 seconds of your time.
How fast do I get my first carousel? +
Within minutes of signing up. RunSound generates your first carousel immediately — you don't have to wait. After that, a new draft appears in your TikTok inbox every day.
What images does it use for the carousel? +
You upload your own photos, or we generate a custom image library using AI — visuals that match your song's vibe and genre. All images are unique to your campaign.
How does the AI know which hooks work best? +
Every carousel gets a unique UTM link connected to your Spotify. RunSound tracks which posts actually lead to streams, then uses that data to make the next carousel smarter. It learns your audience over time.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Cancel anytime, no questions asked. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
